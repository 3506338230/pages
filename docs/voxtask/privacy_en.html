VoxTask Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-09-22

This Privacy Policy explains how VoxTask ("the App") handles your information. Please read it carefully before use.

1. Who we are

  • Product name: VoxTask (voice memos to text)
  • Contact: 3506338230@qq.com

2. What we collect and how we use it

We only process information necessary to provide “speech-to-text” and related features.

  • Microphone audio (user content): Captured only when you actively press and hold to record and start transcription. Audio is sent via encrypted connection (TLS/WSS) to a third‑party ASR service (ByteDance ASR) for transcription. The App does not store your audio locally or on our own servers for the long term.
  • Transcribed text: Stored only on your device (App sandbox) for display and saving as memos; not uploaded to our own servers.
  • Configs & credentials: Sensitive configs (ASR token, LLM API key) are kept in iOS Keychain and used only to connect to their respective third‑party services; not uploaded to us.
  • Diagnostics: Used locally for troubleshooting (e.g., error codes, network timeouts). By default no audio content is included; not collected by us remotely.

3. Third‑party services & sharing

To enable speech recognition and (optional) text analysis, the App communicates with:

  • ByteDance ASR (openspeech.bytedance.com, WSS) — audio is sent to obtain transcription.
  • Optional LLM provider (e.g., GLM‑compatible) — only when you enable “smart analysis/polish”, transcribed text is sent to get suggestions.

Beyond the above, we do not sell or share your data for advertising purposes.

4. Security in transit

  • All communications with third‑party services use encrypted transport (HTTPS/WSS/TLS).
  • We recommend using trusted networks to avoid handshake failures or risks caused by proxies/inspection.

5. Storage & retention

  • Audio: not retained locally or on our own servers; sent only for real‑time ASR processing.
  • Text: stays on your device until you delete/clear or uninstall the App.
  • Credentials: stored in iOS Keychain; you can clear them in Settings (debug builds).

6. Your rights

  • Access, correct, and delete data saved inside the App (e.g., delete tasks/clear credentials).
  • Revoke permissions (e.g., disable microphone, clear credentials in Settings).
  • Contact us at 3506338230@qq.com.

7. Children

The App is not directed to children under 13. If you believe we processed children’s data unknowingly, contact us so we can act promptly.

8. Changes

We may update this policy due to feature or regulatory changes. Updates will be posted here and take effect from the “Last updated” date.
